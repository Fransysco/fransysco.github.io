Fresh 48

Fresh 48 is done within the first 24-48 hours of your newborn's life. I will come to the hospital or your home and do a more lifestyle session which will not involve much posing. Parents and siblings are encouraged to participate in this session. This is best to be booked while pregnant.

Please contact me to schedule your Fresh 48 session! Make sure to include your expected due date.
