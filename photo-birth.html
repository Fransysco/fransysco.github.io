Birth Photography

Birth photography is truly amazing. Many women do not remember parts of their birth so looking back at pictures of your baby's first breath is a good way to reminisce. Pictures include labor, delivery, and one hour after birth. I will attend local hospital, home, and Buffalo Birthing Center births. I will be on call two weeks before and two weeks after your due date. This should be booked sooner rather than later in your pregnancy in case baby decides to be born earlier than expected.

Please contact me to schedule your birth session! Make sure to include your expected due date.
